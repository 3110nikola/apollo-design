Meet the team

MARAT ANISIMOV

Founder & Digital Director

Marat co-founded Apollo Design with Marty Lane in 2004 and now guides the company’s technical direction. As an expert in usability and user centred design, he has led large-scale UX projects, focussing on speed of innovation, usability and service design in the NHS and other public sectors.

You could describe Marat as an obsessive road cyclist, former outdoor pursuits instructor and owner of a dog called Surprise.

MARTY LANE

Founder & Creative Director

Marty is a co-founder of Apollo Design and a highly skilled visual designer with an extensive background in service design for online health services. He brings a freshness and clarity of vision to everything he does with expertise in both visual design, information design and user-centred design processes.

You could describe Marty as a non-fiction book-lover, inspired bin-the-recipe cook, sans serif font aficionado and eternal seeker of inspiration.

CLAUDIO STABILE

Lead developer

Claudio started his career as a graphic designer but found his passion was ultimately in writing code. For the last 10+ years Claudio has found his forte creating websites, extranets and apps for international clients with a heavy focus on public sector and private healthcare clients.

You could describe Claudio as a music loving wannabe mountain biker that loves cats and playing video games.

ANN ELLIS

Account Manager

Ann joined Apollo as a Project Manager after spending ten years working in retail management. She is a perpetual perfectionist and prides herself on her great communication skills that ensure clients are always bang up-to-date with progress on their projects.

Animal-loving Ann has pet cats and guinea pigs called Kit & Kat and Scooby & Shaggy respectively.

You wouldn’t dare describe Eddie as anything other than what he tells you to say – he is a 4th degree black belt in kung fu after all. On top of this he is an avid sci-fi book addicted guitar playing computer gamer. He told me to say that.
